Parties Involved

There are three distinct roles in the system:

1. Direct Customers (B2C)

Definition
End customers who buy flight tickets directly from you.

How tickets are sourced

You can obtain tickets for direct customers via:

Airline companies directly, or

Third-party vendors (other agencies)

2. Agents (B2B)

Definition
Agents are resellers who sell tickets to their own clients but purchase tickets from you.

How tickets are sourced

You obtain tickets from:

Airlines directly, or

Third-party vendors

Financial responsibility

‚úÖ Agent owes you

‚úÖ You owe the airline or vendor

3. Vendors (Other Agencies / Suppliers)

Definition
External suppliers from whom you purchase tickets to resell.

Role

Financial responsibility

‚ùå Vendors never invoice customers or agents

1. Sales Invoice (Accounts Receivable)

Customer OR

Agent

Key Accounting Rules (IMPORTANT)
